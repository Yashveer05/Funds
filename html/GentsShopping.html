About Organozation


“ Remember that the happiest people are not those getting more, but those giving more.”

Our idea is to design a system that will enable people to donate clothes that can either be donated further to needful people to use or sent for recycling. From the clothes that’ll be segregated for recycling bags of different types will be made. The account of all the data regarding the number of clothes that were collected from the donations, number of the onessent for reuse and the remaining sent for recycling, the number of bags and their costs and other data related to the delivery, reports, etc will be maintained using a database. The bags made from recycling clothes will then be made available for selling on the site. The money generated from these transactions will be provided as funds to various social welfare Organizations.
